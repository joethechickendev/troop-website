Why 349?

When you make the decision to join a troop you need to know how it works and how it accomplishes the goals set forth by the BSA. Below are some reasons why we believe you should choose our troop:

High Quality

Our troop is known for our trained leaders, our balance of scouting ranks, and our extensive planning. We plan all events, including campouts and fundraisers, up to 18 months in advance, and our merit badge meetings cycle every 2 years. Since 2010, 60 Eagle Scouts have graduated from our troop.

Activities

We plan for and go on 10 camping trips, regularly participate in community service events , merit badge events, and day trips every year. We also attend area overnight scout camps and high adventure camps, as well as weekly Tuesday Meetings at the American Legion.

Scout Run

We believe in the concept of “boy led” with the addition of the phrase “parent mentored” to teach leadership to boys in a safe and encouraging environment. Part of our mission is to give every boy a safe environment to learn and practice the principles of leadership.
